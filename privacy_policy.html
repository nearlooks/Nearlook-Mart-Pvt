1. Overview


Near Look is committed to protecting the privacy of its clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services or visit our website.


2. Information Collection


Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, postal address, or phone number. This information is collected when you fill out forms on our website or communicate with us.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit. This data helps us understand how users interact with our website to improve its functionality and user experience.


3. Use of Information


Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services, as well as to communicate with you regarding updates, promotions, or service-related matters.

Communication

We may use your contact information to send you marketing or promotional materials, but you can opt out of receiving such communications at any time.


4. Data Sharing and Disclosure


Third-Party Services

We may disclose personal information to third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our clients. These third parties are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose personal information when required to comply with legal obligations or enforce our policies to protect the rights, property, or safety of Near Look, our clients, or others.


5. Data Security


Protection Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Retention Period

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.


6. User Rights and Choices


Access and Control

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. You can do so by contacting us using the details provided in this Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.


7. Policy Updates


Modifications

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our data practices. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates.
